Events

HackO'Clock

It will be 6-hours Hackathon which will challenge participants on a variety of technology stack including Development and Deployment of Apps. A pen-paper based MCQ will be conducted to select the teams for the final round.

Alo-Homora

It is basically a treasure hunt event where the participants need to go throughout the campus in search of clues. To join the race the participants need to clear a priorly conducted pen-paper based test.

Ad-Hogs

In this event a one-minute ad is to be made by the participants on the provided brand. Use of any device or software is permitted to make your ad shoot presentable.

CodeKnight

This is an online 2-hours coding competition starting from 10 p.m. A HackerEarth link would be provided to the participants on the second day of the event.

Inquizitive

It is mainly a technical quiz event comprising of questions from various domains of Information and Technology. It is a 3 round affair starting from a pen-paper based test moving on to sequential and Buzzer round.
